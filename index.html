PERSE - Workout Builder & Assignment
(React, Node.js, Express, CSS3, HTML5)
My dad is a former Olympic speedskater, and is still involved with U.S. Speedskating as a coach and mentor for young athletes. He is not impressed by the internet. Which means when he writes training programs, they're often filed on scraps of paper, scribbled in ballpoint pen. So I built this app to make it easier on his kids, with an easy system for writing, assigning, and retrieving workouts. It's built with ReactJS, Node.js, Express, and PostgreSQL, using a RESTful API architecture with endpoints that support GET and POST operations.

DIBS - Calendar Holds Manager
(React, Node.js, Express, CSS3, HTML5)
I help run a small live music venue in Milwaukee, and managing the process for holding dates on our calendar is an irritating and time-consuming process, at best. This form and back-office solution gathers hold requests from artists, agents, and private parties, and instantly informs the user of their hold order for each date requested on submission. A matching back office solution displays holds placed in the database, giving the administrator the ability to change the hold status and automatically populate email to the contact on the hold, and then filter by date and status.

Sewart Real Estate Group Website Redesign
(JQuery, JavaScript, CSS3, HTML5)
Sewart Real Estate Group is a First Weber Realtors agency located near Milwaukee, specializing in luxury residential properties on the North Shore. I designed and produced this site end-to-end, and wrote all of its copy and marketing collateral. Property listings are generated entirely from the broker's front end using vanilla JavaScript, HTML and CSS to sidestep the hassle of dealing with antiquated MLS/IDX data, which saved the business several thousand dollars in development costs. It also includes a custom mortgage calculator that suggests properties within the user's budget.
